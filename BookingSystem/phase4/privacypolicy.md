# Privacy Policy for the Booking System

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-13

This Privacy Policy explains how our Booking System collects, uses, and protects your personal data. We are committed to processing your data in compliance with GDPR principles.

## 1. Data We Collect

We only collect data necessary for the functionality of the booking system:

*   **Identity Data:** Email address, Password (hashed).
*   **Reservation Data:** Resource booked, Start time, End time.
*   **Demographic Data:** Birthdate (used only to enforce the 15+ age requirement for booking).

## 2. How We Use Your Data

*   **Service Provision:** To register your account and manage your bookings.
*   **Access Control:** To verify your role (Reserver or Administrator) and enforce age restrictions (Spec 6).
*   **Security:** To protect the system against unauthorized access.

## 3. Data Minimisation and Privacy by Design (PbD)

*   **Identity Disclosure:** We are designed **NOT** to display the identity of the resource reserver on the public booking schedule (Spec 8). *However, please refer to the security audit, which highlights a current failure in this implementation.*
*   **Data Retention:** We retain your account data only as long as you use our service. You may request account deletion at any time.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user, you have the right to:

1.  **Access:** Request copies of your personal data.
2.  **Rectification:** Request correction of any inaccurate data.
3.  **Erasure ('Right to be Forgotten'):** Request that we delete your personal data.
