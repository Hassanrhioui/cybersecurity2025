# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-13

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are and how we use them on our Booking System website.

## 1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your browser to store information about your activity.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We use only **Strictly Necessary Cookies** (also known as Essential Cookies).

*   **Session Management:** We use a cookie named `session_id` to maintain your login state (i.e., to remember that you are a Reserver or an Administrator). This cookie is essential for the security and functionality of our access control system.
*   **CSRF Protection:** We use a cookie named `csrf_token` to protect against Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) attacks.

## 3. Cookie Types

*   **Strictly Necessary:** These cookies are vital for the application to function securely and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you, such as logging in or submitting forms.
*   **No Tracking/Analytics:** We do **not** use any cookies for advertising, tracking, or external analytics (e.g., Google Analytics).

## 4. Managing Cookies

You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but parts of the site (such as logging in or making a reservation) will not work.
